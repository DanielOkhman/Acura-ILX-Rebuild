## Objective
To restore a damaged 2019 Acura ILX to full operational and cosmetic condition while developing hands-on mechanical, fabrication, and project management skills. This project focused on diagnosing structural damage, repairing the vehicle frame, and returning the car to safe driving condition. All in a time and cost effective manner. 

---



## Project Overview
This project involved rebuilding an Acura ILX that had sustained structural damage. The repair required frame alignment, welding structural components, electrical repair, and final reassembly.

The project served as an opportunity to apply engineering-style problem solving to a real mechanical system while managing the repair process from diagnosis to completion.

---

### Skills Learned/Refined

- Structural inspection and damage assessment  
- MIG welding for automotive frame repair  
- Mechanical system troubleshooting  
- Metal preparation, cutting, and reinforcement techniques  
- Safe use of power tools and shop equipment  
- Project planning and staged repair workflow  
- Wiring repair with proper soldering technique
- Identifying missing or damaged compenents and replacement 
- Quality control and final inspection procedures  


---

### Tools Used
- MIG welder
- Angle grinder
- Metal cutting tools
- Socket and wrench sets
- Jack stands and hydraulic jack
- Iron Solder
- Voltmeter
- Safety equipment (welding helmet, gloves, etc.)

---

### Steps Taken

1. **Initial Inspection**
   - Evaluated the extent of structural and mechanical damage.
   - Identified frame sections requiring reinforcement or welding.

2. **Disassembly**
   - Removed damaged components to expose the structural frame.
   - Prepared the work area and ensured safe access to repair locations.

3. **Frame Preparation**
   - Cleaned and ground metal surfaces to prepare for welding.
   - Removed damaged metal and aligned structural members.

4. **Frame Welding**
   - Reinforced and welded structural sections of the frame.
   - Ensured weld strength and proper alignment.

5. **Reassembly**
   - Reinstalled removed components.
   - Checked mechanical systems and fasteners.

6. **Electrical**
   - Identify all plugs
   - Fix or replace wiring
   - Connect all electrical componenets
  
7. **Final Inspection**
   - Verified structural integrity and vehicle functionality.
   - Performed visual inspection and testing to confirm successful repair.
---

## Results

- Successfully restored the vehicle to operational condition
- Reinforced structural areas affected by damage
- Developed practical fabrication and automotive repair skills
- Demonstrated the ability to complete a complex multi-stage mechanical project
